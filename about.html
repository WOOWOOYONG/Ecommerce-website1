ONE OCEAN, ALL LANDS

WHO WE ARE?

Being lovers of the ocean, we were looking out at the Shonan sea one day, imagining Hawaii out there beyond the Pacific Ocean and California further beyond, when the realization hit us, “the ocean is one and the world is connected.” I told this idea to the founder of the New Zealand outdoor brand “Macpac,” Bruce McIntyre (who is also my brother in law) and he came up with the phrase “One Ocean, All Lands” for us. Taking these words as our mantra, we would like to continue supporting a peaceful way of living based on the philosophy that sees the world as one.

Taking “UTILITY” and “SPORTS” as key words, nanamica mixes fashion and functionality at a high level to offer “standard wear for the current age” with neutral design that transcends the boundaries of genre, age, or gender.

ONE OCEAN, ALL LANDS. The ocean is one and the world is connected.
